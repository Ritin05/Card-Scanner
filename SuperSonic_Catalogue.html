Who We Are

Company Profile

One of India's largest Import & Export trading companies for industrial machinery products

Building India's Industrial Future with World-Class Technology

Super Sonic was established in 2003 and has grown to become one of the largest Import & Export Trading Companies in India. Headquartered in Surat, Gujarat, our state-of-the-art facility spans 42,000 sq. ft, housing a comprehensive inventory of industrial machinery products sourced from the finest Chinese and international manufacturers.

We are dedicated to serving High Quality and Cost-effective Products to our customers. Our extensive range covers Industrial Automation, CNC Machine Components, Laser Machinery, Mechanical Parts, Cutting & Welding Systems, and more. With hundreds of satisfied customers across India and internationally, we believe in long-term business relationships built on trust, quality, and mutual growth.
